Land RegistryDesign Manual

Based upon the GDS design manual, this is a manual for making digital services, for both public facing services, and for internal applications that support them.

Who is this manual for?

This manual's primary purpose is to met the needs of Designers.

How will it help me?

Design better things, more quickly. Services and applications everyone can have confidence in.

Confidence that what is made meets a standard - either the GDS Service Standards for public services, or comparable standards for internal applications. Standards that are not just opinion, but shown to meet user needs/solve problems.

What is in the manual?

Currently - 11th November 2012 - very little. The plan is to, as designers, discuss what we want this manual to achieve, and make it together.
